About Me

I’m from Great Falls, Montana. I’m currently attending the University of Montana, where I’m majoring in Media Arts and earning certificates in Web Design and Graphic Design. I’ve always loved art and creativity, and my goal is to become a professional graphic designer. I also hope to sell my artwork on the side.
